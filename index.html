About this project

The AI-Powered Trash Collecting Boat is an innovative autonomous system designed to identify and remove floating waste from water bodies. Using intelligent vision and automated collection, it helps keep lakes and ponds clean with minimal human effort. This aims to offer a smart, eco-friendly solution for reducing water pollution and promoting a healthier environment.
